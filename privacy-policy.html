Privacy Policy for “Find Bono”

Last updated: 2025-05-09

1. Introduction

“Find Bono” (“we”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store your data when you visit our site.

2. Data We Collect

  • LocalStorage: game start timestamp, bonus clicks, medal counts, streaks, last‐played date.
  • Cookies: none by default (only if you consent to analytics below).
  • Analytics: page‐view data via Google Analytics (if you opt in).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To persist your daily play state and stats.
  • To compute streaks, medal tallies, and game lockouts.
  • Only with your consent: to collect usage metrics (“Do you accept analytics cookies?”).

4. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Clear cookies/localStorage via your browser settings.
  • Withdraw analytics consent; this stops any further analytics data collection.
  • Request deletion of any stored data by emailing privacy@findbono.com.

5. Analytics Opt-In

When you first arrive we ask:

“We use cookies to measure traffic. Accept to help us improve.”

If accepted, we load Google Analytics; if declined, we never set any analytics cookies.

6. Third-Party Services

— Google Analytics
— Web Share API

7. Data Retention

All localStorage data persists until you clear it. Analytics data is retained per Google’s standard policy.

8. Contact Us

Email: privacy@findbono.com
